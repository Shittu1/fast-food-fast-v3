About us

Fast Food Fast is an online fast food delivery store with focus on very fast delivery of ordered fast food and at the same time not compromising quality. Fast Food Fast will serve the young, middle age and elderly people of Lagos metropolitan.

We offer for sale a wide variety of fast food and we also render home delivery services too. Contact us if you have any questions.
